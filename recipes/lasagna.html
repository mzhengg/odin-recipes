Lasagna Recipe

Description of the dish

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, tomato sauce, and cheese. It can also include ground meat, vegetables, and herbs. The layers are typically repeated multiple times and baked until the top is golden and crispy. The result is a hearty and flavorful casserole that is perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
